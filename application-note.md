Application Note: Causal Structuring in Decision Systems

This note applies the CFS (Cognitive Flow System) framework to illustrate how decisions in complex environments emerge through structured causal constraints rather than direct enforcement.

The model demonstrates that outcomes are not imposed, but shaped through sequential loops that progressively limit available options.
